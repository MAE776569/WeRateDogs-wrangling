Conclusions¶

  • About 75% of our data set is perdicted to be dogs
  • The max rating was given is 1776 and the min is 0
  • The mean favorite count for dogs is 8781.578983516483 and for non dogs 7588.950787401574
  • The mean retweet count for dogs is 2525.543956043956 and for non dogs 2516.3307086614172
  • Based on the visualizations there is no significant difference between favorite count betweem dogs and non dogs and the same for the retweet count
  • The most favorited tweet was the most retweeted with favorite_count 159245 and retweet_count 79478
  • About 94% of the tweet was tweeted from twitter for iphone
